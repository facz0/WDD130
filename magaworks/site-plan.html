Content

My site is a place where the people can be free to view all the cakes or desserts that my girlfriend can make. If the people want to order some desserts for a meeting, party, family meeting, or any event, they will come to my site because here is the place where they going to find that. Also, my site has an easy way to contact personally with the pastry chef. In the same way if the people have a customize cake for a birthday or a married, they can order in my site talking with the pastry chef. The goal is that the people can easily ask for the thing they want and want to return every time that they want to order. I hope that the people can see all easy. I want that they can find and view the list of things without restrictions. I want to have all that they don’t have doubts. If they want prices, I going to give them prices. If they want to know if she has a restaurant, I will let them know. Also in the future I want to put a place where the people can ask something not necessarily talking personally with the pastry chef, something like a blog.
Talking about the thinks that people can do in the web-site. The first thing that you can do is to know about the life and experiences of the pastry chef. You can see her age, her knowledges, how she learns, where she actually works and more. Another thing is that you can have contact with the pastry chef, asking for your order. In this section you will see the list of desserts and different examples of cakes and customized cakes for to be easier to choose to you. You will have and idea about what you can order. Also, you will see the price. Another thing is her social media. There is a section of pictures of her and her cakes. Navigate is so easy, you can read and enter in any part that you want without get lost. We will need just your personal information like name and phone number for take the order and no more.
I think that people have to visit my site instead other sites because first, they will find delicious things and very beautiful designs for them customized cakes. The experience of to eat these desserts is unique. The strong attractive of my site is the experience of the people with the food. If the people want eat or order more, they will enter more often to mi site. The site is pleasing to the eye, the colors and design are nice. This project or idea came to my mind because I want that the people see the work of my girlfriend and my work. She works in a great place; she is the boss but her dream is to be independent. And I am actually a web developer student. I am learning how to make web-site and one of my goals and dreams since I study this career is make a website for my girlfriend. I want people know our work and progress with the time. I am going to update this page as I learn more also for the people for them commodity and them comfortable navigation. I hope people enjoy this work and help us to grow. I want this page be the best for their necessity.
